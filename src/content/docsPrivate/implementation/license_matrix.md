Greenlight Approvals is offered in three license tiers: **Core**, **Pro**, and **Enterprise**.  
This guide summarizes the features available in each tier and highlights what users can expect as they upgrade.

## 1. Tier Overview

Each Greenlight tier is designed for a different stage of organizational maturity:

- **Core** – Essential approval automation for small to mid-sized teams.  
- **Pro** – Adds budget intelligence, SoD warning, and advanced email features for growing finance departments.  
- **Enterprise** – Full compliance suite including SoD enforcement, unlimited audit exports, integrations, and premium support.

## 3. Version & Licensing Notes

**License Behavior**
- Features are enabled automatically based on license tier.  
- Changing tiers updates feature availability in real time.  
- Existing workflows do not break during tier changes.

**Upgrade Path**
- Upgrading between tiers does not require reinstalling the SuiteApp.  
- All data (rules, steps, logs, drawer content) remains intact.
