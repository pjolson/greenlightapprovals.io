## 1. What Greenlight Is

Greenlight Approvals is a modern, high-performance approval automation system for NetSuite.  
It replaces Workflows, SuiteApprovals, and custom logic with a centralized, auditable, intelligent decision engine.

Greenlight provides:

- Fast, reliable, enterprise-grade approval workflows  
- A unified Approval Drawer inside every transaction  
- Budget and AI contextual insights  
- Advanced SoD protection  
- Delegation and exception handling  
- A full Command Center for managers and finance leaders  
- Audit-ready logs and export packages  

Greenlight becomes the authoritative approval layer for all financial transactions.

## 6. Competitive Landscape

### 6.1 SuiteApprovals

**Differences**
- No SoD  
- No budget insights  
- Limited logs  
- Difficult configuration  

### 6.2 ApprovalMax

**Differences**
- External to NetSuite  
- No inline Drawer  
- No true SoD  
- No audit export parity  

### 6.3 Custom Workflows

**Differences**
- Hard to maintain  
- Slow and error-prone  
- Not auditable  
- No governance or analytics layer
