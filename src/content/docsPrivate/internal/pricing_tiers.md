This internal document summarizes Greenlight’s pricing model, positioning strategy, and tier capabilities.  
It is intended only for internal planning, sales enablement, and strategic alignment.

## 1. Overview

Greenlight’s pricing is designed to balance:

- Accessibility for SMB and mid-market NetSuite customers  
- Strong value differentiation at higher tiers  
- Predictable recurring revenue  
- Clear upgrade paths into Pro and Enterprise  
- Alignment with features that materially increase compliance value  

Pricing tiers align directly with feature clusters.

---

## 2. Pricing Philosophy

### 2.1 Value-Based Tiering

**Principles**
- Core provides essential workflow automation  
- Pro adds financial intelligence (Budgets + AI)  
- Enterprise adds compliance, audit, and SoD enforcement  

### 2.2 Expansion Revenue Strategy

**Approach**
- New dashboards, compliance tooling, and AI upgrades land in Pro/Enterprise  
- Core remains stable and accessible  
- Enterprise pricing reflects audit and governance value  

### 2.3 Competitive Positioning

**Notes**
- Priced below ApprovalMax + integration work  
- Significantly more capable than SuiteApprovals  
- Includes unique features (SoD, Budget Intelligence, Drawer UX)  

---

## 3. Tier Definitions

### 3.1 Core Tier

**Target Customer**
- SMB to mid-market  
- Low compliance requirements  
- Needing a modern Drawer + rule engine  

**Includes**
- Approval Rules & Steps  
- Approval Drawer  
- Details, Timeline, Comments  
- Basic logs  
- SLA dashboard (basic)  
- Email approvals  
- Basic exception detection  

**Goal**
- Replace SuiteFlow/SuiteApprovals reliably  

---

### 3.2 Pro Tier

**Target Customer**
- Growing finance teams  
- Needing spend intelligence  
- Wanting better decision support  

**Adds**
- Budget Intelligence Engine  
- Budget variance block in Drawer  
- Vendor & transaction AI insights  
- Budget dashboards  
- Exception dashboard  
- SLA thresholds + critical amounts  
- Advanced Report Settings  

**Value**
- Makes approval decision-making smarter  

---

### 3.3 Enterprise Tier

**Target Customer**
- Audit-heavy organizations  
- SOX/SOC2 environments  
- Multi-entity businesses with strict governance  

**Adds**
- Full AI Insights (multi-period, contextual)  
- SoD enforcement  
- Delegation  
- Audit Package export  
- Trend dashboards  
- High-value transaction monitoring  
- Extended exception categories  
- Additional Command Center modules  

**Value**
- Compliance, audit assurance, and governance integrity
