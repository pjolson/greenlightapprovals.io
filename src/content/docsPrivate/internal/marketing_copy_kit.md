This internal Marketing Copy Kit provides ready-to-use messaging for website pages, emails, social posts, demos, and collateral.  
It is NOT customer-facing documentation — it is a library of approved language for consistent brand and product storytelling.

- “Approvals that finally make sense.”
- “Modern approvals for NetSuite.”
- “The approval engine built for finance teams.”
- “Faster approvals. Stronger controls.”
- “Smarter decisions inside NetSuite.”

### 1.2 Positioning Statements

**Primary**
- “Greenlight is the modern approval automation engine for NetSuite — replacing SuiteFlow and SuiteApprovals with a faster, more intelligent, more reliable system.”

**Secondary**
- “Greenlight centralizes approvals, adds financial intelligence, and delivers audit-ready governance inside NetSuite.”

## 7. Internal Messaging Guardrails

- “Audit-ready”  
- “Budget Intelligence”  
- “SoD enforcement”  
- “Approval Drawer”  
- “Queue-based reliability”

- “Workflow builder replacement” (not yet)
