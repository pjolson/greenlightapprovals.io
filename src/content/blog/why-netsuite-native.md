When approvals live inside NetSuite instead of a separate tool, approvers stay in context. No switching to another tab or app—the tray slides out from the transaction they’re already viewing.

That means fewer mistakes, faster decisions, and a single audit trail. We’ll walk through why native integration beats bolt-ons for finance teams.
